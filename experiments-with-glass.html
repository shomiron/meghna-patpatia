Experiments with Glass and Found Objects

Magnifying glass, tools used for examining and preserving natural resources. The act of looking and engaging with a natural element through a magnifying glass almost encases the aspect of the element being caught inside a memory which I create through mixed mediums onto diverse surfaces. The creating of a memory caught in a circular goblet is explored with experiments using found objects and other elements.
