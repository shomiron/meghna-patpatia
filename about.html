Artist Statement

My works are detailed ink drawings that explore creatures and natural forms synergising and adapting to manmade interventions in dystopic environments.

The study of creature behaviour is inspired by mythological, historical and faerie elements. Along with these I draw an imagined future by connecting all the epochs bygone and currently unfurling. Sentient beings and natural elements are constant witnesses to changes in our biodiversity and terrain. I believe in honouring them by drawing them in diverse environments with repetitive patterns weaving them into transformative shifts.

The phenomenon of Transverse orientation, where all living organisms are drawn to light created a curiosity in me to study creature behaviour. Wherein consciously and unconsciously sentient beings are drawn to light, like a moth to flame. To this there is a parallel drawn of natural resources disappearing because of manmade intervention in the name of progress. My art practice reflects these aspects of co-existence and depletion through the dreamscapes I create.

I experiment with various materials – ink, handmande papers, textiles, adhesives, found objects magnifying glass, tools used for examining and observing. The act of looking and engaging with a natural element through a magnifying glass almost encases the aspect of an element being caught inside a memory. which I create through mixed mediums onto diverse surfaces.

Along with my own art practice, I also dapple in various other art related pursuits like art therapy and art restoration.
