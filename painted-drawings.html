Painted Drawings

This medium has its roots in my experience while working in the field of art restoration, where textile was used to line old paper manuscripts for strengthening. I further experimented with the same materials and stumbled upon a raw terrain of handmade veined papers which are pasted onto translucent textiles to create a nerve like pattern which I draw on with ink and water. My painted drawings are created combining traditional art practices with a surreal essence of morphism to reflect a sensation of movement and adaptation.
